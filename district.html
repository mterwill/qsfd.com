Monthly Meeting

The Board of Fire Commissioners meets the 4th Monday of each month at 7 pm, with the exception of October when the meeting will be held on Tuesday, October 18th at 7pm. In addition, a 2nd monthly Commissioners’ meeting might be held, immediately following the Quaker Springs Fire Department monthly meeting at approximately 7:45pm, only if needed for urgent agenda items.

Meetings are held at the main firehouse on Blodgett Road off Route 32 in the hamlet of Quaker Springs.

All meetings of the Board of Fire Commissioners are open to the public. Members and the public are encouraged to attend.
