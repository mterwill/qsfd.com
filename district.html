Monthly Meeting

The Board of Fire Commissioners meets the 3nd Monday of each month at 6 pm, with the exception of October when the meeting will be held on Tuesday, October 15th.

Meetings are held at the main firehouse on Blodgett Road off Route 32 in the hamlet of Quaker Springs.

All meetings of the Board of Fire Commissioners are open to the public. Members and the public are encouraged to attend.
