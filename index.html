Quaker Springs Fire District #1
March 27, 2023 PUBLIC HEARING

Notice is hereby given that a public hearing will be held by the Quaker Springs Fire District #1 on March 27, 2023, at 7:00 p.m. at the Quaker Springs Firehouse, 107 Blodgett Rd, Schuylerville, NY in the Town of Saratoga. The purpose of the public hearing is to discuss the support and passage of a new resolution allowing a partial property tax exemption (to the extent of 10% of the assessed value of property owned by the firefighter and used as their primary residence) for members of Quaker Springs Fire Department that have been active members for a minimum of 2 years as authorized by New York Real Property Tax Law ยง 466-a. This program has been developed by New York state to encourage the recruitment and retention of new volunteer firefighters. All interested parties are invited to attend the hearing. Those who require special accommodations are requested to contact the Fire District Secretary at firedistrict@qsfd.com in advance of the hearing. DATED: March 20, 1023

Thomas Tyler
Secretary/Treasurer

The Quaker Springs Fire Department has been proudly protecting life and property in the Town of Saratoga since 1947. We are a 100% volunteer organization and we are always looking for additional help. If you would like to join our men and women in providing this valuable service, please contact us at (518) 584-3349 or come to a drill at Station 1 any Thursday night.
