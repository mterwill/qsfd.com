Quaker Springs Fire District #1
March 27, 2023 PUBLIC HEARING

NOTICE is hereby given that a public hearing will be held to discuss the support and passage of a new Town Law allowing a partial property tax exemption (to the extent of 10% of the assessed value of property owned by the firefighter and used as their primary residence) for members of Quaker Springs Fire Department that have been active members for a minimum of 2 years as authorized by New York Real Property Tax Law ยง 466-a. Such hearing will be held Monday, March 27th, 2023 at 7:00 p.m. at the Quaker Springs Firehouse, 107 Blodgett Rd, Schuylerville, NY in the Town of Saratoga. Anyone interested in attending the meeting should contact the Fire District Secretary at the email listed below for details.

Thomas Tyler
Secretary/Treasurer

firedistrict@qsfd.com

The Quaker Springs Fire Department has been proudly protecting life and property in the Town of Saratoga since 1947. We are a 100% volunteer organization and we are always looking for additional help. If you would like to join our men and women in providing this valuable service, please contact us at (518) 584-3349 or come to a drill at Station 1 any Thursday night.
