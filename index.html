Quaker Springs Fire District #1
December Commissioner’s Meeting Rescheduled

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Board of Fire Commissioners of Quaker Springs Fire District #1 of the Town of Saratoga, County of Saratoga, New York has rescheduled its Monday, December 26th, 2022, monthly meeting to Wednesday, December 21st, 2022, at 7:00pm at the Firehouse at 107 Blodgett Road, Quaker Springs, NY. All meetings of the Quaker Springs Fire District Board of Fire Commissioners are open to the public.

Thomas Tyler
Secretary/Treasurer

firedistrict@qsfd.com

The Quaker Springs Fire Department has been proudly protecting life and property in the Town of Saratoga since 1947. We are a 100% volunteer organization and we are always looking for additional help. If you would like to join our men and women in providing this valuable service, please contact us at (518) 584-3349 or come to a drill at Station 1 any Thursday night.
